About Me

My name is Chiamaka Anyanwu, a passionate Computer Science professional and advocate for technology-driven education in Africa. A graduate of Babcock University, where I earned my degree in Computer Science, I have cultivated deep expertise in Cloud Administration and Engineering, and actively contribute to the advancement of tech communities.

I served as the lead for the Google Developers Group on Campus Cloud track at Babcock University, where I led initiatives that empowered fellow students through workshops, collaborative projects, and professional development events.

I have also worked in other student leadership positions and volunteering positions as well.
